📅 The Google Calendar extension that puts your schedule one click away: check Google Calendar from any tab, create events, send a Google Calendar invite, RSVP, and join video meetings, all from your browser toolbar, without opening a new tab.\n\nClick the toolbar icon (or press Alt+C) and your day, week, or month appears instantly. It is the fastest way to answer \"where is my Google Calendar?\" while you keep working on whatever page you are on.\n\nWHY THIS CHROME CALENDAR\nMost people either keep a Google Calendar tab open all day or lose track of their schedule entirely. This chrome calendar addon solves both: a compact popup that works like a Google Calendar widget and behaves like a computer desktop calendar, always within reach. No separate download or desktop client required: sign in once, and this lightweight Chrome plugin brings your full schedule into the toolbar. The popup opens in a fraction of a second and closes the moment you click away, so checking what is next never breaks your flow.\n\nKEY FEATURES\n• Day, week, and month views — a compact calendar tool with a live now indicator and smart grouping: Happening Now, Coming Up, Later, Completed.\n• Quick add — create an event with title, date, time, duration, and guests in seconds; every Google Calendar invite is sent automatically, making Google Calendar appointment scheduling a two-click job.\n• One-click meeting join — the extension detects Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams links in your events and shows a Join button right on the event card.\n• RSVP from the popup — accept, decline, or reply Maybe to invitations; the full Google Calendar app stays closed.\n• Calendar reminder done right — desktop notifications before events, an optional badge with a countdown to your next event, and Focus Mode for when you need quiet.\n• Everything in one place — show or hide each of your schedules individually, with colors matching Google Calendar™.\n• Google Calendar dark mode and more — a proper dark theme for late hours, plus light and warm themes, simple or advanced toolbar, and adjustable display size.\n• Works offline — recently loaded events stay available even without a connection.\n• Speaks your language — 52 languages, automatic time zone handling, and keyboard shortcuts for fast navigation.\n\nHOW TO SHARE A GOOGLE CALENDAR\nWondering how to share a Google Calendar without digging through settings? This is a genuinely shareable calendar app: open the sharing panel, enter an email address, choose view or manage rights, and the Google schedule share is done. The other person sees your events in their own app, and revoking access is just as easy. It works as a shared calendar app for a team or a family calendar app for the household, with no extra service in between.\n\nHOW TO USE\n1.
