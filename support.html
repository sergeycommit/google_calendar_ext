Top fixes users need most often

I do not see my calendar events

Check that you are signed in to the right Google account, refresh the popup, and confirm the extension still has Calendar access.

Notifications are not showing up

Verify browser notification permissions, then make sure reminders are enabled inside the extension settings.

I cannot join a meeting from the popup

Make sure the event actually contains a Meet, Zoom, or Teams link and check whether a popup blocker is interfering.

The wrong Google account is active

Switch accounts inside the extension and confirm that the desired accounts are already logged into Chrome.

Quick setup checklist

1 Install and pin

Add Schedule Calendar from the Chrome Web Store and pin it so it stays one click away.

2 Grant access

Accept the permissions prompt so the extension can read your Google Calendar events.

3 Check settings

Review notification timing, account selection, and any other preferences that affect your daily workflow.

Before emailing support: refresh Chrome, reopen the popup, and check whether your events appear correctly in Google Calendar on the web. That often narrows the problem immediately.

Detailed troubleshooting

Events not loading

Sign out and back in, refresh the popup, and verify Calendar permissions in Chrome.

Notifications missing

Enable browser notifications, then confirm reminder settings inside the extension.

Meeting button not working

Check the event details for a valid link and temporarily disable popup blockers.

Extension loading issues

Restart Chrome, update the browser, and temporarily disable conflicting extensions.

Fastest path to help

For technical issues or account-specific problems, email support and include what you already tried plus a short description of what the popup is showing.
